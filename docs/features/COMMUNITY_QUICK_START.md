## How to Use

### For Participants

#### Start a Discussion
1. Go to any event page
2. Scroll to "Community" section
3. Click "Discussions" tab
4. Click "Start Discussion"
5. Write your message
6. Click "Post"

#### Ask a Question
1. Go to any event page
2. Scroll to "Community" section
3. Click "Q&A" tab
4. Click "Ask Question"
5. Enter question title and details
6. Click "Post"
7. Wait for organizer to answer

#### Upload a Photo
1. Go to any event page
2. Scroll to "Community" section
3. Click "Photos" tab
4. Add caption (optional)
5. Click "Choose Image"
6. Select your photo
7. Done! Photo uploads automatically

#### Reply to Discussions
1. Click on any discussion or question
2. Type your reply in the text box
3. Click send button

#### Like Photos
1. Hover over any photo
2. Click the heart icon
3. Click again to unlike

---

### For Organizers

#### Pin Important Discussions
1. Click the pin icon on any discussion
2. Pinned posts appear at the top with yellow border

#### Answer Questions
1. Reply to any question in Q&A
2. Your reply is automatically marked as "ANSWER"
3. Question gets "ANSWERED" badge

#### Moderate Content
1. Delete any inappropriate discussions, comments, or photos
2. Use trash icon on any content
